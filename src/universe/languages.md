# Languages

## Akina

Akina (Ak-i-na) is the language spoken within the [Kingdom of Bradford](./kingdoms.md#bradford) in varies in dialects. It's based on Germanic languages and has heavy emphases on pitch-accept tones. Notably, that *i* in Akina sounds like short *e*, as in bed, while pitches have their own dedicated letters, รก and รณ, respectfully.
